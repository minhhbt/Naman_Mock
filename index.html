CONCEPT DESIGN

Naman Residences exemplify elegance through simplicity.

The integration of lush gardens, open interiors with natural elements creates harmony for residents to enjoy the beauty of the location from the privacy of your villa.

Incorporation of natural elements of wood, bamboo and stone complement the clean lines and open air design. Interiors include modern amenities and fittings to optimize the highest standard of living.

DESTINATION

Danang has become Vietnam’s “most ideal” holiday destination. With an excellent international airport, pristine white sand beaches and cultural World Heritage sites, Danang offers visitors a unique and memorable experience unmatched throughout the world.

Naman Retreat is a secluded, beachfront enclave nestled between two of the finest golf courses in Southeast Asia - Greg Norman’s Danang Golf Club and The Montgomerie Links. The world-famous Hoi An Ancient Town is just moments away, whilst the resort gives easy access to Danang City, The Marble Mountains, Ba Na Hills entertainment and the World Heritage Sites of My Son Sanctuary and the Imperial City of Hue.

OWNER'S BENEFITS

Naman Residences is an integrated part of Naman Retreat. Owners will have the privilege of enjoying our top-notch class public facilities such as pools, restaurants, bars, spa, and kids club.

Villas are fully furnished with all 5-star standard furniture and fixtures with high Committed Return on Investment rate.

Owners will benefit from our attractive rental programs operated by Naman Retreat.

Owners will be entitled to privileges such as 20% discounts on public rates for accommodation, F&B services, spa, and gym.

Owners will be entitled to permanent Ownership Certificate with long term land use right for Vietnamese and 50 years plus applicable extension for Foreigners.

RENTAL PROGRAM

"The Developer will operate Rental Program for Villa Owners by putting Naman Residences Villas into Naman Retreat's inventory for regular rental purposes. Villa Owners are entitled to 40 days complimentary use of their Villa annually (terms and conditions applied) with exclusive owner benefits plus a 85% share of Rental Profit of the Unit after operating expenses and applicable fees. The Developer guarantees that such Rental Profit will not be lower than 5% of the unit contract price (In Vietnam dong before Value Added Tax) per annum during the first 10 years of Rental Program."
